Welcome to my website

ianlong Song is currently a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Michigan State University. His research primarily focuses on broadband communications, anti-jamming techniques and navigation. Meanwhile, he possesses the knowledge of algorithms, machine learning and computer networks. In his spare time, he has interest in a wide variety of sports, like swimming, basketball, soccer, pingpang, tennis, badminton, etc.
