Welcome to my website

ianlong Song is currently a software development engineer in the Data Science and Engineering team at Zillow. He received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Michigan State University. His interests are primarily focused on software engineering, big data platforms and artificial intelligence. He has the knowledge and experience with machine learning, data mining, natural language processing and game theory. In his spare time, he has interest in a wide variety of sports, like swimming, basketball, pingpang and badminton.
