Welcome to my website

ianlong Song is currently a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Michigan State University. His research interests can be summarized into two categories: one in brain neuroimaging data mining, and the other in wireless communications. He has the knowledge and experience with machine learning, data mining, natural language processing and game theory. In his spare time, he has interest in a wide variety of sports, like swimming, basketball, pingpang and badminton.
